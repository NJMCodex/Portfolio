Codex Economy

An economy mod for Minecraft 1.21.

The goal of Codex Economy is to give the player the real world experience of a fully functioning economy system. The mod adds debit cards that are directly linked to the the players checking account, credit cards with set credit lines, and a custom ATM that allows players to both deposit and withdraw money notes that are kept in the players wallet.
